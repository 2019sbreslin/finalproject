To use this helpful too, just enter up to five rounds of golf. We will only count the ones that are filled in. Your 18 hole score is what you put down on your score card and

the course rating is something every course has and is usually found on your scorecard or course's website.Lastly, the course slope is also something found on every card or website!

Why is this tool so important?

Trying to calculate your handicap is not something you can figure out using quick math in your head. There are actually USGA specific rules which determine exactly how a golfer is to calculate his or her USGA Handicap Index®.

If you want to enter a prestiges event or tournament, you will be required to tell the officials your handicap so they know if you're qualified to play this tournament.
